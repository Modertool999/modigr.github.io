Video Game Recommender

Paste your SteamID64, set three weights, and get a ranked list of games. Steam is a popular PC gaming platform by Valve; this app uses Steam data to personalize picks.

    Signals look ~0. If your profile or recent games are private (or you haven’t played recently), personalization won’t work. Raise Content weight or make playtime visible, then retry.

    How it works

    • TF-IDF over top games’ name/description/genres.
    • Pulls your + friends’ recent playtime via Steam Web API.
    • Scores with weighted: content similarity, friends’ playtime, friends’ similarity.
